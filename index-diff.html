2) Research Approach

So nutze ich das Influencer Research Tool

  1. Seed Search: Kategorien (Sex-Ed, Dating, Relationship, Feminism), Keywords, Hashtags
  2. Audience Fit: DACH-Share, Sprache, Demografie, Interests, “Audience Overlap” mit CHEEX Persona
  3. Brand Safety: Content Tonalität, Kommentar-Kultur, “Red Flags”, Plattform-Historie
  4. Performance Signals: ER, Story-Views, Reels Completion, Link-Click Patterns
  5. Shortlist: 15–25 Creator → final 3, plus 2–3 Backups
Fallback falls Tool dünn ist: IG “Suggested”, Creator Collabs, Podcast/Newsletter Creator, TikTok-Discovery (nur als Input), manuelles Comment-Sentiment-Sampling.
2 Beispiele

Zwei konkrete Research-Workflows

  • Approach A — Keyword → Segment:
    “Beziehung”, “Kommunikation”, “Dating” → filter DE-Audience → Shortlist → Story-Format-Fit prüfen
  • Approach B — Lookalike/Overlap:
    Seed Creator (ähnliche Werte) → Audience Overlap → “Adjacent Communities” → Backups definieren

Output Eine Shortlist, die “Brand Fit” priorisiert, nicht nur Reichweite.

6) Influencer Briefing

Briefing: Guardrails, nicht Mikromanagement

Must-Haves

  • Value-first: Education/Insight im Vordergrund
  • 7-Tage Trial CTA (Link + Code) klar sichtbar
  • 1 Reel + Story Sequenz (Poll/Q&A optional)
  • 1 Review Runde (Script/Storyboard) + Final Check

Don’ts (Safety)

  • Keine expliziten Visuals / “porn-adjacent” framing
  • Keine medizinischen Claims
  • Kein “shock value” — Fokus: normalisierend & respektvoll
  • Keine beleidigenden/abwertenden Kommentare “anheizen”
7) Content Moderation & Compliance

Meta-konform bleiben — konkrete Schritte

Pre-Check
  1. Wording-Checkliste (sex-ed, non-explicit)
  2. On-Screen Text/Thumbnail clean
  3. “Privacy & Safety” Proofpoints
Review & Publish
  1. Script/Storyboard Review (1 Runde)
  2. Final Cut Check (Text/Visual/CTA)
  3. Publish Window + Backup Caption
Moderation
  1. Pinned Comment mit “Safe Space” Regeln
  2. Keyword/Spam Filter (Creator Settings)
  3. Escalation: Hide/Restrict/Report
Fallback bei Issues: Re-Cut (On-Screen Text), Caption austauschen, repost innerhalb vereinbartem Zeitfenster.
8) Performance Evaluation Plan

KPIs & Optimisation Loop (ohne Overkill)

Funnel KPIs
  • Awareness: Reach, 3s View, Completion
  • Intent: Link CTR, Landing CTR
  • Primary: Trials
  • Quality: Trial→Paid, Refund/Cancel signal
Decision Rules
  • Scale: CTR & Trials über Median → mehr Story Slots / Zusatz Reel
  • Iterate: CTR ok, Trials low → CTA/LP/Offer anpassen
  • Stop: Brand fit issues / underperform + no fix → Budget re-route
Timing
  • Woche 1: Launch + baseline messen
  • Woche 2: Hook/CTA Iteration
  • Woche 3–4: Scale Winners, cut Losers
Tracking Setup

Messbarkeit ohne Overkill

  1. UTM-Link pro Creator / Placement
  2. Creator Code (für Story + Kommentar)
  3. Weekly Snapshot: CTR, Trials, Trial→Paid
Warum so? Genug Signal für Entscheidungen, ohne Setup-Debt. Fokus bleibt auf Creative-Iteration.
Reporting

Was ich im Interview zeige

  • 1-Pager “Campaign Health”: Spend, Trials, CPA-Trial, Trial→Paid
  • Top Creative Insights (Hook/Angle/CTA)
  • Next actions: scale/iterate/stop + Budget shift
Bonus: Qualitatives Feedback aus Kommentaren & DMs → “why it worked” dokumentieren.
